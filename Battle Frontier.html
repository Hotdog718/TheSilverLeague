The Silver League
~Battle Frontier~

Just like the games our league has its very own Battle Frontier as well, where familiar and new faces now as Frontier Brains will prepare their own unique sets of rules and play styles to offer every challenger some great fun! Remember that every single Battle Facility has different rules so make sure to give them a read.

Each Frontier has their 4 difficulty rankings. Beginner, Advanced, Master and Legend.Each time you clear one of the tiers, you'll advance to the next one.

-Beginner:
No legends can be used from either side.

-Advanced:
Every Pokemon from our league's modified OU tier and below is fair game.

-Master:
Uber tier Pokemon are allowed.

-Legend:
Every frontier hosts this difficulty.Only the frontier may use Ubers while the challenger can not.Your reward for overcoming this hurdle will be an Iv Bred of the Frontier Brain's Mascot (You may obtain this reward only once).

To challenge a Frontier Brain you must pay a challenge fee with medals,remember that you must keep track of your own medals.

It's important to keep in mind that,similarly to gyms,you may challenge each Frontier Brain once per day when they're available.

On a similar note Mini-Games will be available when the Frontier Brain opens up their facility as well.
