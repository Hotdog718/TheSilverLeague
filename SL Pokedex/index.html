How to use

You can use the up and down arrow keys to change the pokemon (Up = next, Down = Previous) or you can click/tap the two buttons on the bottom left corner of the dex.
If the pokemon has alternate forms you can use the left and right arrow keys to change the form or click/tap forms.
Press S or click/tap on the sprite to change it to shiny and back.
You can press Shift or click/tap on the weight/height to change it from metrics to impearial.
