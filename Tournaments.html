~The SL Monotype Tournament~

Hello Silver League, Dave here hosting the Monotype tournament, rules are actually a bit more complicated than you’d expect. So please pay attention:

1. You may choose your type.
2. You are free to change your team or type between battles.
3. Dual type Pokemon can be used on both types. For example, Gengar can be used on mono-Poison or mono-Ghost.
4. In the case of a Pokémon that can Mega Evolve, both it and its Mega Evolution must share a type with the rest of the team. For example, Regular Gyarados is a Water / Flying type and may be used on mono-Water or mono-Flying teams. However, Mega Gyarados can not be used on mono-Dark or mono-Flying. It can only be used on mono-Water.
5. Monotype is not OU. Certain Ubers with be allowed and certain OU Pokemon are banned
Aside from the Pokemon, rules will follow standard SL clause. Sleep clause, No evasion abilities or moves, Species Clause (different forms aren’t different Pokemon, you get one Deoxys)
6. Have Fun.
