Chatbox Rules and Code of Conduct

[Click to Show]

  • Do not spam the chat, this includes with emoticons or noises. An offense will result a kick, further offenseses will result in a ban.
  • Do not fill your name with emoticons. If a mod asks you to remove emotes from your name, comply or you will be banned.
  • No threatening of violence is allowed.
  • Excessive use of language is frowned upon, keep things civil.
  • Be respectful of the mods and owners. Picking fights or backtalking will not be tolerated.
  • Do not put mods or owners on ignore.
  • Do not interrupt a mod or an owner dealing with a situation.
  • Do not feed the trolls. Joking around while a mod or owner is trying to contain them is highly discouraged and makes things worse.
  • Impersonating another member will result in a ban.
  • Do not advertise other leagues.
  • Be civil to other members in the chat. Disrespect and poor sportsmanship will be punished.
  • Do not spoil gym teams.
  • Do not harass gym leaders for a battle. You are permitted to ask them once.
  • Counter-Teaming is prohibited.
  • Show respect to your opponent, and do not brag about a victory.
  • Forfeiting is frowned upon.
